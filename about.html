About

Built on legacy. Operating for today.

Chief Security Solutions is a clean-slate Alabama LLC carrying forward a family legacy of protection. Licensed in Alabama, Tennessee, Texas, Georgia, Florida, Arizona, and Mississippi, with short-term contracts and executive-protection coverage available nationwide. Chief is co-led by Triston Parsons and Brad Haynes.

Origin

A family trade, restarted on its own terms.

The protection trade runs in founder Triston Parsons’ family. His grandfather served as Chief of Police in New Orleans and Birmingham, then started a private investigation and security firm after returning to Birmingham in the mid-1980s. That standard of care is what Chief carries forward today.

Chief Security Solutions is the next chapter by Triston Parsons and Brad Haynes, designed for the way protection work is delivered today: discreet, professional, technology-enabled, and accountable.
