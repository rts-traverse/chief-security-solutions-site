FAQs

Common questions, answered.

Don’t see your question? Send us an inquiry — we respond within one business day.

  • How quickly can you deploy a detail?
    For routine guard or surveillance engagements, we can typically scope and stand up coverage within 48–72 hours. For urgent executive-protection requests, we can mobilize same-day in our licensed footprint and coordinate nationwide travel within 24 hours. Call our 24/7 dispatch line at (205) 946-7938 for time-sensitive needs.
  • Where are you licensed to operate?
    Chief Security Solutions is licensed for guard, surveillance, and executive-protection services in Alabama, Tennessee, Texas, Georgia, Florida, and Arizona. Executive-protection details travel nationwide on engagement, with proper coordination through local authorities and partner firms where required.
  • Do you publish pricing?
    No — every engagement is scoped to the assignment, so pricing depends on coverage type, detail size, location, duration, and risk profile. Once we understand your requirements, we provide a transparent written quote within one business day.
  • Are your officers armed?
    We staff both armed and unarmed officers, matched to the threat profile and client preference. All armed personnel hold current state armed-guard credentials and complete recurring qualification on use-of-force, de-escalation, and weapons handling. We’ll recommend the appropriate posture during scoping.
  • Do you handle one-time events or only ongoing contracts?
    Both. We staff weddings, conferences, private parties, film productions, and other one-off events alongside our long-term residential, commercial, and EP contracts. Tell us your dates and we’ll scope a fit.
  • Can you integrate with my existing camera or alarm system?
    Yes. We can layer 24/7 remote monitoring onto most existing CCTV, alarm, and access-control hardware, or design and install a fresh system end-to-end. During scoping we audit what you have, identify any gaps, and recommend the smallest change that gets you to full coverage.
  • How do you vet your officers?
    Every officer clears a full background check, drug screen, and license-status verification before their first shift. EP agents additionally undergo reference checks with prior detail leaders and are matched to assignments based on prior protective experience. We don’t subcontract to operators we haven’t personally vetted.
  • Are you insured?
    Yes. Chief Security Solutions carries general liability, professional liability, and workers’ compensation coverage that meets or exceeds state requirements in every jurisdiction we operate. Certificates of insurance are available to clients on request during scoping.
